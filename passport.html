Passport Renewal and Application

PASSPORT RENEWAL AND APPLICATION

You can have your expiblue or missing passport renewed/replaced at the Consular section at the Sierra Leone Embassy in Rabat, by downloading and filling the passport form.
Passport renewal should be worked on within 6 MONTHS before expiry. You are advised to desist from waiting until your passport expires before beginning the renewal process.
Students with missing passports are requiblue to present a Missing Report “Declaration de Perte” from the police station, to the Consular section at the mission in order to get a new passport.

Requirement Checklist:

  • Completed Passport form [Click here to download passport form]
  • Two 2 standard passport size photographs taken full face on a plain background within six months of the date of application, without dark glasses or hat
  • Previous Sierra Leoneian passport issued to you
  • Declaration de Perte (in the case of a missing passport)

Current prices for passport application are as follows:

  • Renewal (36 pages) – 1300 MAD
  • New passport for first time – 1300 MAD
  • Lost / Damaged passport – 2600 MAD

Please visit the Sierra Leone Embassy Morocco Consular Section for more information on how to apply for a Sierra Leoneian passport.
